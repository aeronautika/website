What is Aeronautical Engineering?

Aeronautical engineering focuses on aircraft design, aerodynamics, propulsion, structures, and avionics.

Aerodynamics

Lift, drag, thrust, and airflow around wings.

Structures

Strong, lightweight airframes designed to handle stress.

Propulsion

Piston engines and turboprops used in GA aircraft.

General Aviation Aircraft

Common training and personal aircraft used around the world.
