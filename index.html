What is Aeronautical Engineering?

Aeronautical engineering covers aircraft design, aerodynamics, propulsion, structures, and avionics.

Aerodynamics

Lift, drag, thrust, airflow around wings.

Structures

Strong, lightweight airframes designed to handle stress.

Propulsion

Piston engines and turboprops used in GA aircraft.

General Aviation Aircraft

Common training and personal aircraft used worldwide.

How to Start Learning Aviation

  • Study basic physics and mathematics
  • Learn aircraft systems and performance
  • Use flight simulators
  • Read POHs and AFMs
  • Visit airfields and flight schools
