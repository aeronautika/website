Discover Aeronautical Engineering & Aviation

A beginner-friendly introduction to how aircraft fly, how they are engineered, and why general aviation aircraft form the foundation of aviation.

What is Aeronautical Engineering?

Aeronautical engineering focuses on the design, construction, testing, and operation of aircraft. It covers aerodynamics, structures, propulsion, avionics, and flight mechanics.

Aerodynamics

How air flows around wings and produces lift.

Structures

How aircraft remain strong while staying lightweight.

Propulsion

Piston engines and turboprops used in GA aircraft.

General Aviation Aircraft

General aviation aircraft are widely used for flight training, personal travel, and engineering fundamentals.

How to Start Learning Aviation

  • Study mathematics and physics
  • Learn aircraft systems and performance
  • Use flight simulators
  • Read POHs and AFMs
  • Visit airfields and flight schools
